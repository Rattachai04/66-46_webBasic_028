Cruel Carrot Project. Background of story

This project originated from my passion for drawing. When my sister and her boyfriend undertook their graduation project, I had the opportunity to contribute as one of the assistants responsible for character design and voice acting for a duration of 2-3 months. Throughout this period, we devoted significant effort and dedication to ensure the success of the project. The primary software I utilized for character design was Procreate, after which I meticulously assembled all the components to create scenes intended for animation using Adobe Animate. Our collective commitment and hard work ultimately resulted in the creation of this final piece.
